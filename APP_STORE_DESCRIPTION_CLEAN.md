Transform your spiritual practice with DivinePrayers – the modern way to learn and master Hindu prayers, mantras, and devotional songs.

DivinePrayers makes learning prayers accessible, engaging, and meaningful. Whether you're a beginner or looking to deepen your practice, our app combines traditional wisdom with modern technology to help you learn at your own pace.

COMPREHENSIVE PRAYER LIBRARY

Access a growing collection of sacred prayers including:
- Hanuman Chalisa – Complete 40 verses with meaning and explanation
- Hanuman Aarti – Devotional songs for daily worship
- Hanuman Baan – Powerful verses for protection and strength
- Gayatri Mantra – Ancient Vedic mantra for enlightenment
- And more prayers being added regularly

Each prayer includes the original text in Devanagari script, English transliteration, detailed meaning, and thoughtful explanations to deepen your understanding.

- Multiple-choice questions for key verses

Build a consistent spiritual practice with our daily verse system:

- Customizable daily notifications
- Progress tracking shows total verses learned

BILINGUAL AUDIO PLAYBACK

- Word-by-word highlighting as audio plays

Perfect for auditory learners and those who want to learn correct pronunciation. Each verse plays in Hindi first, followed by English translation and explanation.
