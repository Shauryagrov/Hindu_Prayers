## Best Practices

1. **No Spaces After Commas** - Saves characters
2. **No Duplicates** - Each keyword appears once
3. **Mix Broad + Specific** - Balance discoverability with targeting
4. **Include Brand Terms** - "hanuman", "chalisa" are searchable
5. **Feature Keywords** - "bilingual", "quiz" highlight unique features
6. **Avoid Generic Terms** - "app", "free" don't help (already implied)
7. **Use Singular Forms** - "prayer" not "prayers" (App Store handles plurals)
8. **Test and Iterate** - Update based on search analytics

---

## What NOT to Include

❌ **App name** - "DivinePrayers" (already indexed)
❌ **Generic terms** - "app", "free", "best"
❌ **Competitor names** - Don't include other app names
❌ **Redundant terms** - "prayer" and "prayers" (use singular)
❌ **Very long phrases** - Keep keywords short
❌ **Special characters** - Stick to letters and commas

---

## Testing Strategy

1. **Initial Launch:** Use recommended primary option
2. **After 30 Days:** Check App Store Connect analytics
3. **Identify:** Which keywords drive downloads
4. **Optimize:** Replace low-performing keywords
